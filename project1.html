The Hangman PlayBook

McMaster Design League: Tabletop Toy Competition

Project Overview

Designed for a tabletop toy competition, our team created a fully portable "PlayBook." The concept was to transform a standard-looking book into a functional Hangman game board upon opening.

Key Engineering Features

  • Integrated Storage: Designed internal compartments to house the hangman body parts and gallows stand.
  • Mechanical Hinging: Engineered a "living hinge" or mechanical fold that allows the book to sit flat during gameplay.
  • Prototyping: Developed the 3D model using SolidWorks [cite: 9] to ensure all parts fit with precise tolerances for 3D printing[cite: 9].

Team Contribution

As a team contributor[cite: 5], I assisted in the mechanical layout and 3D modeling of the components, ensuring that the assembly was functional and met the competition's design constraints.
